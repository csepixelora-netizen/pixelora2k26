About Symposium

A Legacy of Innovation

PIXELORA 2K26 is a flagship symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, bringing together innovation, creativity, and technical excellence.


Built on the vision of "A NEW BIRTH," it provides a platform for students to explore, showcase, and innovate. Join us in redefining the boundaries of the digital world.

Technical Events

Push the Boundaries

Innopitch

Pitch the impossible. Convince the inevitable.

Share your new idea to solve a real problem in simple and clear steps.

  • Team of 1–3 including the leader (solo, duo, or full trio)
  • 3-min pitch + 2-min Q&A
  • Original ideas only
  • Venue: Main Block Auditorium
  • Best 3 performers awarded
Register →

DevFolio

Don't say your skills. Show them.

Develop a portfolio (on-spot scenario) involving real-world coding challenges.

  • Individual participation
  • On-spot scenario dev
  • Must involve coding
  • Venue: CSE Main Lab
  • Best 3 performers awarded
Register →

PromptCraft

One prompt. Infinite imagination.

Create AI-generated visuals using a single, carefully crafted prompt.

  • Individual participation
  • 2 rounds (20 mins each)
  • Single prompt generation
  • Venue: Multimedia Lab
  • Best 3 performers awarded

Non-Technical Events

Fun Meets Strategy

E-Sports

Game hard. Dominate the battleground.

Compete in high-intensity multiplayer gaming matches where strategy, reflex, and teamwork decide the winner.

  • Custom lobby match
  • 4 members per team
  • Fair play rules strictly followed
  • Top 2 teams win
Register →

Channel Surfing

Switch fast. Guess smart. Win quick.

A fun clue-based event where participants identify TV channels, shows, logos, or scenes in rapid rounds.

  • Team of exactly 2 people (1 leader + 1 teammate)
  • Rapid-fire visual rounds
  • Quick clue identification
  • Best 3 teams awarded
Register →

IPL Auction

Bid smart, build strong.

Slots Left: 10

  • Team of exactly 4 (1 leader + 3 teammates) | Budget: 100C
  • Squad: 15 (11 Play + 4 Sub)
  • Overseas slots: 6 | Max 4 in 11
  • Bid smart, build your team
Register →

Visual Connect

Find the link. Crack the connection.

Find the common connection between given images or clues.

  • Team of exactly 3 people (1 leader + 2 teammates)
  • Identify the correct connection
  • Time limit for each question
  • No mobile phones allowed
  • Judge's decision is final
